Road Quality Assessment

During the second and third years at Télécom Physique Strasbourg, students have to create groups of 3 to 5 in order to work for a company for a year. The goal is to train students for working as a team in a company with a customer.

With two classmates, we formed a team in order to work for Alcatel-Lucent Enterprise on a road quality assessment project. The initial goal was to create a solution to quickly detect road degradations by analysing accelerometric data with artificial intelligence... 🏎️💨
