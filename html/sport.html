I have nerver had any problems with running but it took me some time (17 years to be precise 😅) to discover I actually love running! 🏃
I started with 15 minutes joggings, improved little by little, until I was able to run for an hour. As my passion began to expand, I started to go faster and I quickly wanted to go farther. I am now able to complete a marathon at a good pace. But I will keep on training until I can finish ultra-trails! 👟
As you may have guessed I prefer to run in nature, but I also like to go slower sometimes and enjoy beautiful landscapes during hikes. 🥾
I would also like to start riding bikes again: I know for a fact I really enjoy technical mountain bike riding but I would definitely like road bike as well. 🚵🏼‍♂️
Other than that: swimming during summer and skating and skiing in the winter! ⛸
